Chris Forster

I am associate professor in the Department of English at Syracuse University. My research focuses on censorship and obscenity, modernist literature, and the history of media. If the first pair those terms are of interest to you, you may be interested in Filthy Material: Modernism and the Media of Obscenity (Oxford UP, 2018). If the latter pair, you might be interested in Modernism and Its Media (Bloomsbury, 2020).
